Frequently Asked Questions

What vaccines does my pet need?

Common vaccines include rabies, distemper, and parvovirus. Consult our vet for a tailored plan.

How often should I bring my pet for a checkup?

We recommend a checkup every 6 months for adult pets and more frequently for puppies/kittens.

What should I feed my pet for a healthy diet?

A balanced diet includes high-quality pet food, fresh water, and occasional treats. Avoid human food that may be toxic.

Do you offer emergency veterinary services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care. Please call us immediately in case of an emergency.

What are the signs of a sick pet?

Signs include loss of appetite, lethargy, vomiting, diarrhea, or unusual behavior. Contact our vet if you notice any of these symptoms.
